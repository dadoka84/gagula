Lawyer

Mr. Sc. Almir Gagula
Born in Konjic 1982.

Almir Gagula is lawyer specialized in corporate, contract and tax law. He has extensive experience as a legal advisor of international corporate clients on the daily basis. List of the clients includes different companies such is largest car producer within Europe and one of the largest in the World, one of the leading tobacco companies, well known European train producer, one of the largest milk production companies as well as leading German retail chains selling clothes for youngsters and shoes.

Almir is also permanent arbitrator in Arbitral court of Foreign trade of Chamber of Bosnia and Herzegovina. He is also active at Faculty of Law on University of Sarajevo where he holds classes within course of Obligation law.
Almir holds degree of Magister of Science in Contract law as a top graduate student in generation with flawless average mark 10/10. In 2009 he defended summa cum laude his master thesis Legal remedies of termination of the contract under Bosnian law and Principles of European Contract law. He immediately continued with his PhD dissertation Contractual freedom as an instrument of autonomous regulation which is currently in final stage of preparation.
He is one of the few lawyers specialized in tax law where he has extensive experience that is strongly related to family background and software development (Basic, Pascal, SQL) experience in his young age.

Halil Gagula
Born in Konjic 1955.

Halil Gagula is lawyer specialized mostly in civil and corporate law. He has over 35 years of practice as in-house counsel, head of legal affairs, judge and president of Municipal Court in Konjic, and as a lawyer in last 15 years.

Halil has been working for large number of companies and individuals solving different types of legal challenges. He has been working on preparation of privatization in dozens of companies.
